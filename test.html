What Is HRT

Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) is an option for men and women whose testosterone and hormone levels are not as high. Women comprise around 80% of patients with an imbalance of hormones, and men are responsible for 20 percent. Women can experience hormonal loss between 30 - 40. Men may experience symptoms of low testosterone in the 40s to 50.

Hormone Importance

The human body's hormone system is crucial in almost every one of bodily functions. Hormones are a communication channel for all cells within our body. They assist in growth in digestion, sexual health reproductive system, immune system, cerebral health, and many other things. When hormones become in a state of imbalance, no matter how minor the imbalance, the entire body is affected.
